Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

VANVASI is designed to work entirely on your iPhone. We do not operate servers for core app functionality and we do not require an account.

Data on your device

  • Your free-app allowlist (Phone, Messages, VANVASI, etc.)
  • Lock on/off state and unlock session history
  • Focus statistics (score, streak, unlock count)
  • Optional PIN (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • Payment metadata if you enable optional paid unlocks (Apple processes payment)

What we do not collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
  • No cloud sync of your data
  • No access to content inside other apps (messages, browsing, etc.)

Apple Screen Time API

VANVASI uses Apple's Family Controls framework to apply app shields. Per Apple's design, Screen Time and blocking data remain on your device.

Notifications

VANVASI may send local notifications when you choose to unlock a blocked app from the system shield, so you can complete the unlock flow in the app.

Children

VANVASI is intended for users who choose to lock their own device. It is not designed as parental monitoring software.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. The effective date will be updated on this page.
