Contact

Email us: support@vanasi.app

We typically respond within 2–3 business days.

Getting started

  1. Complete onboarding and grant Screen Time permission.
  2. Select Phone, Messages, and VANVASI as free apps.
  3. Tap the lock ring to enable monk mode.

Locked out?

Open VANVASI → tap ⋯ (top right) → End lock. If PIN is enabled, enter your PIN.

System fallback: iPhone Settings → Screen Time → turn off VANVASI restrictions.

Shields not working?

  • Use a physical iPhone (not Simulator).
  • Confirm Screen Time permission is granted.
  • Ensure Family Controls entitlement is active on your build.

Requirements

iOS 17 or later. iPhone only.
