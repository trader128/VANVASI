# VANVASI Privacy Policy

**Effective:** July 2026

VANVASI respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely on your iPhone without requiring an account or cloud service.

## What we store on your device

- Your free-app allowlist (Phone, Messages, VANVASI, etc.)
- Lock on/off state and unlock session history
- Focus statistics derived from your usage of VANVASI
- Optional PIN (hashed, stored in iOS Keychain)
- Payment records if you enable optional paid unlocks (metadata only; payment processing is handled by Apple)

## What we do not collect

- No user accounts
- No analytics or advertising SDKs
- No cloud sync of your data
- No access to content inside other apps (messages, browsing history, etc.)

## Apple Screen Time API

VANVASI uses Apple's Family Controls framework to apply app shields. Per Apple's design, Screen Time data remains on your device.

## Optional payments

If you enable "Pay to unlock" in Settings, purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. We do not receive your payment card information.

## Children's privacy

VANVASI is intended for users 13+ who choose to lock their own device. It is not designed as parental monitoring software.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
